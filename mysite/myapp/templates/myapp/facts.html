Facts & Stats

Goal:

Offer competitively priced energy and help customers reduce bills through flexible tariffs and efficiency tools.

Invest heavily in renewable energy generation to drive the green transition. The goal is to supply 100% renewable energy.

Evolve its Kraken platform and products like OctoFi to lead innovation in energy tech.

Accomplishments:

Octopus Energy has major office presences in the UK, Germany, US, Australia, Spain, Italy, France, Japan and New Zealand

Reached unicorn status in 2021 with a valuation over $2 billion. Currently valued at approximately $3.4 billion.

Founder Greg Jackson awarded an MBE and named in Fortune 40 Under 40 list in 2022.

Fun Projects:

Electric vehicle propositions - Offers specialized Octopus EV tariffs, along with options to install home EV chargers. Also has a partnership with Tesla to provide discounted electricity for Powerwall users.

Octopus Co-op Solar - A joint venture with Co-op Energy to install rooftop solar panels and batteries on Co-op Food stores to generate clean energy.

Hydrogen Trials - Pilot projects to test injection of hydrogen into the gas grid in partnership with SGN.
